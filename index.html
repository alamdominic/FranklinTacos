Our Story

Salsa Franklin Taco is a family-owned food truck in Franklin, TN, bringing the authentic spirit of Mexican street tacos with a modern, urban, and elegant twist.

We believe in quality ingredients, traditional recipes, and the warmth of Mexican hospitality. Every taco is crafted with love and passion to bring you an unforgettable culinary experience.
